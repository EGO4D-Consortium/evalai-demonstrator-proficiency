Evaluation and Prizes

Submissions will be ranked as per the evaluation criterion in the Evaluation tab above. To be considered for awards, participants must submit a report describing their method, data used for training/evaluating their approach, details of the model architecture, training/testing paradigm as applicable, and analysis of different aspects of the model. The report will be due within a week after the challenge ends and would have to be submitted using the workshop CMT portal. Top-3 submissions that outperform the baselines, submit a report and satisfy all terms and conditions will be eligible for the following prizes:

  • First prize: $3000
  • Second prize: $2000
  • Third prize: $1000
